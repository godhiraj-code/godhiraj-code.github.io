 A book that every golang developer should read.

Title is appropriate as with golang is always doing it first and doing it in correct way. Do Less But Enable More


First chapter introduce you to why go on first place and why do we need go now more than ever.

Chapter Two - (word of caution for beginners) - Read it but revisit it after reading chapter 3 to 7.   Chapter two will straightaway go through the advanced concepts in go and one may not be able to understand it for the first time, Please re read it once you are through chapters 3 to 7. 

Rest of the chapters will try to take you to the minds of Rob pike, Ken Thomson , Robert Griesemer and why they did what they did.
At least it tries to and as one go through the pages , one will be amazed by the intelligence of authors and core go developers.

Chapter 6 and 7 goes through the concurrency and communication sequential process philosophy which is one of the most interesting parts of the book.

By the end of chapter 9 which is on Testing and Benchmarks , one will know the importance of Testing in any , even a trivial program .

Happy Programming
